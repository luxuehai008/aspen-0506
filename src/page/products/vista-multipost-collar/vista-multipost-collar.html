Vista® MultiPost Collar

#984200
PDAC Code L0180 Approved
The Vista® MultiPost Collar is the latest addition to Aspen’s award-winning line of Vista Cervical Collars. This new, fully-adjustable collar with pivoting occipital panels adds an extra level of support for greater motion restriction. Like the original Vista Collar, the Vista MultiPost Collar is one-size-adjustable, virtually eliminating waste associated with collar sizing errors. It also features Aspen’s proven cotton-lined pads designed to enhance skin care by reducing patient contact points in the occipital area.
